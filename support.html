Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI recognition work?

HomeAssetr uses OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision to analyze photos of your items. Simply take a picture, and the AI identifies the brand, model, category, and estimated value automatically. The process takes just a few seconds.

Do I need an internet connection?

Yes, AI recognition requires an internet connection to analyze photos. However, you can browse your existing inventory offline. Your data syncs automatically when you're back online.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your inventory is stored securely in the cloud with encryption. Only you can access your data. We never share your information with third parties.

How many items can I add?

There's no limit! Add as many items as you need to catalog your entire home.

Can I export my inventory?

Yes! You can generate PDF reports of your entire inventory or specific categories. Perfect for insurance claims or moving.

What if the AI gets something wrong?

You can edit any details after the AI scan. The AI provides a great starting point, and you can adjust brand, model, value, or any other field manually.

Can I add items without photos?

Yes! While AI recognition works best with photos, you can manually enter items without using the camera.

How do I delete an item?

Swipe left on any item in your inventory list and tap Delete. Or open the item details and use the delete button.

Is there a subscription fee?

HomeAssetr is free to download and use. Premium features may be available in future updates.

Troubleshooting

AI recognition isn't working

Make sure you have a stable internet connection. Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, contact support.

Photos won't upload

Check that you've granted HomeAssetr access to your photo library in Settings → Privacy → Photos.

I forgot my password

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot Password" to receive a password reset email.
